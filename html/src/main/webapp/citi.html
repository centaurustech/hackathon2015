    There is no minimum to open a checking account in the Citigold Account Package. Monthly service fees are waived for the first two months, and you'll continue to have service fees waived if you maintain:
    • $50,000 or more in eligible linked deposit and retirement balances for the prior calendar month

    OR

    • $100,000 or more in eligible linked deposits, retirement balances, and investments for the prior calendar month
